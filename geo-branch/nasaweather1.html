Perseverance Weather

Rover Daily Weather Report

The Mars Environmental Dynamics Analyzer (MEDA) measures and provides daily and seasonal reports on atmospheric pressure,
humidity, ultraviolet radiation at the Martian surface, air temperature, and ground temperature around the rover. Read more about
the MEDA weather station.
